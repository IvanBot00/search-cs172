Learn more about opportunities to connect below or reach out to the Student & College Engagement Team at careercounseling@ucr.edu. Invite Us to Your Classroom Career Center counselors and peer advisors are available to deliver career presentations to campus groups, classes and student organizations. Submit your request at least one month before the desired program date. We require participation of at least 15 students.  Here are some options of what we offer: Workshops/Presentations on topics such as our overview of services, career exploration, resume writing, and more Employer Information Session (Company/Organization/graduate programs representatives come and share information such as their opportunities and application process) Request a Career Center program. *Note: Programs are requested through a Project Management platform called WorkFront. All communications regarding your request will take place through this platform. If you have any trouble accessing the link, please reach out to the Student & College Engagement Team for assistance. Share Our Programming Career Fairs Open Doors Each year, the Career Center hosts a number of Career Fairs. These are one-day recruiting events for firms in search of candidates for full-time, part-time, and internship positions. Workshops & Information Sessions Every quarter the Career Center offers a variety of workshops, information sessions, and networking events for students geared to help students from beginning to build their resume to those who have begun to plan for life after college. Information sessions allow students to meet and talk to representatives of top companies. Throughout the years there are many formal and informal networking events from coffee chats to etiquette dinners. Check the Career Center calendar for any upcoming events that may address your students’ needs. Connect with Your Career Specialist Each college has a designated Career Specialist to support students, faculty, and staff. We invite you to connect with your Career Specialist to discuss partnerships and programming that would best support your students!
